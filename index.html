Kenzie's Park

Introduction

Hi, my name's Kenzie and I'm a mini Cavoodle. I just turned 3!. My favourite place to visit is Queen's park, which is where I had my first run as a puppy. I always need a nap after we visit the park. When I was younger, I used to be a tri-colour, black hair with ruby and blonde tips. As I got older my colours started to fade and once I had a few haircuts (which I hate!) I turned blonde, but I still have some ruby and black around my ears and tail.

Interests

Most of the time, I like to chill in the shade under the beautiful trees, especially on a hot day. My other favourite place is on the cricket pitch, where my mum throws the ball, but I only bring it back if I get a treat. Sometimes we sit on the bench and I watch the sunset.

Hobbies

My other favourite hobbies are helping my mum work in the study, although I normally fall asleep because it's boring. I also have a neighbour called Frankie and sometimes we go to the beach
